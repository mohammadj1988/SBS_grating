stability map: g₁·g₂ ∈ [0,1] is stable · ● = this cavity

Model. Homogeneously broadened Lorentzian gain centred at 1064.1 nm, FWHM ≈ 120 GHz at 300 K scaling ~linearly with T (phonon broadening); line centre shifts ≈ −1.1 GHz/K. Optical path Lopt = (n−1)ℓ + L with n(T)=1.82 + 7.3×10⁻⁶ΔT and ℓ(T) expanding at α = 7.5×10⁻⁶ K⁻¹, so FSR = c/2Lopt. Threshold compares peak round-trip gain (∝ pump) against round-trip loss −ln(RHRROC)+δ. Transverse modes use the bare two-mirror g-parameters (crystal treated as a thin element). Thermal lens (thermo-optic only): 1/f = ηhP·(dn/dT)/(2κπwp²), ηh=0.30, κ=11 W/m·K, wp=0.35 mm — its effect on stability is reported separately. Values are illustrative of the real scalings, not a substitute for a full ABCD + rate-equation design code.
